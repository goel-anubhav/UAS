Unique AppSites is a YouTube channel that provides educational videos on various topics related to computer science, engineering, and management.

The channel is run by Anubhav Goel, who is a Software engineer and a Content Creator.

The channel also provides updates on AKTU (Abdul Kalam Technical University) exams and circulars for students.

The channel aims to provide quality and informative content for Learners and Enthusiasts.
